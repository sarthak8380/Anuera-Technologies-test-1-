OVERVIEW

Professional Installation & Commissioning

Installation and commissioning are the critical final phases of any automation project — where designs become reality and systems come to life. Anuera Technology provides comprehensive on-site installation, wiring, testing, and commissioning services across India for all types of control panels, automation systems, SCADA platforms, and industrial networks.

Our team of experienced commissioning engineers ensures that every system is installed correctly, configured precisely, and tested thoroughly before handover — minimising downtime and maximising system reliability from day one.

We follow strict methodologies including Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) at our facility before dispatch, followed by Site Acceptance Testing (SAT) during commissioning — ensuring zero surprises during startup.

Our Key Capabilities

📦

Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT)

Complete testing of panels and systems at our manufacturing facility before dispatch, documenting all results.

🔌

On-Site Installation

Professional mechanical and electrical installation of all panels, instruments, sensors, and actuators per approved drawings.

💻

PLC/SCADA Programming & Configuration

Loading and testing of all PLC programs, HMI screens, SCADA configurations, and communication setups on-site.

✅

Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)

Comprehensive loop testing, I/O checks, interlock testing, and full system functional testing before handover.

🎓

Operator Training

Hands-on training for plant operators and maintenance teams to ensure safe and effective system operation.

📋

Documentation & Handover

Complete as-built drawings, operation manuals, maintenance guides, and spare parts lists provided at handover.
